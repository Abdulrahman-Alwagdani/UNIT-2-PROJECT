Infrastructure and Support for eSports in Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia as a Regional and Global Esports Hub

Vision 2030’s emphasis on entertainment and cultural development also intersects with the growth of esports. Esports is not only viewed as a recreational activity, but as a viable avenue for cultural expression and economic growth. Saudi Arabia’s forays into the gaming world reflect an understanding of the global impact of esports, and the potential for the Kingdom to become a regional hub for gaming and esports innovation. HRH Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman bin Abdulaziz Al Saud said of the gaming industry: “We are harnessing the untapped potential across the esports and games sector to diversify our economy, drive innovation in the sector, and further scale the entertainment and esports competition offerings across the Kingdom.”
One of the goals of esports and gaming is to create career pathways to thousands of Saudi Arabian youth who are enthusiastic about gaming and would aspire to find a career path that matches their passion. According to the Chairman of the Federation of Esports and Gaming HRH Prince Faisal bin Bandar bin Sultan Al Saud, the Kingdom’s aim to become a leading global hub for gaming and esports by 2030 will create over 35,000 jobs and contribute $13.3bn to the nation’s economy. Not surprisingly, the growth of the gaming industry has led to job creation, especially in areas such as game development, esports event management, and streaming.

A notable initiative is the establishment of the Saudi Esports Federation and the hosting of major gaming events, showcasing a commitment to nurturing local talent and creating a competitive gaming ecosystem. Additionally, the rise of esports has attracted global sponsorships and partnerships, hence enhancing the country’s international visibility and potentially boosting tourism. These partnerships not only infuse capital into the gaming sector but also foster an environment conducive to further growth.

Multi-Dollar Infrastructure Investments

The development of esports and gaming in Saudi Arabia is a multifaceted endeavor, combining infrastructure investments, educational initiatives, and a strategic alignment with Vision 2030’s broader goals. Investments in gaming infrastructure have played a pivotal role in the industry’s growth, which is expected to have a notable impact on the Kingdom’s economy. These investments in gaming infrastructure, such as the establishment of esports arenas and gaming hubs, can stimulate job creation and contribute to the diversification of the economy. For instance, the ambitious planning and development of a gaming and esports facilities in Qiddiya City, with a 5,155-seat multifunctional venue and six tapering volumes, hopes to eventually host large year-round events and to attract 10 million visitors.

Educational Programs

Esports has become a medium to teach many young and old values that are essential to success in many facets of life—from teamwork and collaboration to problem-solving, persistence, and all-around professionalism. To promote career pathways in esports, the Saudi Esports Academy offers extensive courses on strategies and tactics of different titles. The Saudi Esports Academy is the gateway for esports training, offering esports players the opportunity to enhance their skills, reach professionalism, and then compete in major local and international esports events. It also provides them with knowledge in all areas of esports, to build a professional profile that positions them as future leaders in the esports sector. The academy includes a comprehensive team of professional esports players and a select group of global esports coaches, as well as numerous experts in the field.

The academy boasts:

  • 17 Training Programs
  • 117 Coaches
  • 202 "Become Pro Player" Graduates
  • 197 "eSports Career Training" Graduates
  • 630 Outreach Participants
