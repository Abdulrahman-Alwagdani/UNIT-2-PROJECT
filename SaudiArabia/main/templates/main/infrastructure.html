Saudi Arabia's Strategic Plan for eSports Development

Saudi Arabia as a Regional and Global eSports Hub

Vision 2030’s focus on entertainment and cultural development intersects with the growth of eSports, which is seen not only as a recreational activity but also as a vehicle for cultural expression and economic growth. Saudi Arabia aims to leverage the global impact of eSports to become a regional hub for gaming and innovation.

One key goal is to create career pathways for Saudi youth passionate about gaming. According to HRH Prince Faisal bin Bandar bin Sultan Al Saud, Chairman of the Federation of eSports and Gaming, the Kingdom aims to become a leading global hub for eSports by 2030, creating over 35,000 jobs and contributing $13.3 billion to the economy. The growth of the gaming industry has already led to job creation in areas such as game development, eSports event management, and streaming.

Key Initiatives and Investments

A notable initiative is the establishment of the Saudi eSports Federation, which hosts major gaming events to nurture local talent and build a competitive gaming ecosystem. The rise of eSports has also attracted global sponsorships and partnerships, enhancing international visibility and potentially boosting tourism. These partnerships infuse capital into the gaming sector and foster an environment conducive to further growth.

Multi-Billion Dollar Infrastructure Investments

The development of eSports in Saudi Arabia involves comprehensive infrastructure investments, educational initiatives, and alignment with Vision 2030’s goals. Investments in gaming infrastructure, such as the establishment of eSports arenas and gaming hubs, play a pivotal role in industry growth and economic diversification. An example is the ambitious gaming and eSports facility in Qiddiya City, featuring a 5,155-seat multifunctional venue, which aims to host large-scale events and attract millions of visitors.

Educational Programs at the Saudi eSports Academy

The Saudi eSports Academy plays a crucial role in promoting eSports as a medium for teaching essential values such as teamwork, collaboration, problem-solving, persistence, and professionalism. The Academy aims to create career pathways in eSports by offering extensive courses on strategies and tactics for various game titles.

Training and Development

The Academy provides comprehensive eSports training, enabling players to enhance their skills, achieve professionalism, and compete in major local and international events. It covers all areas of eSports, helping students build a professional profile to become future leaders in the industry.

Faculty and Expertise

The Academy is staffed by a team of professional eSports players, global eSports coaches, and numerous experts in the field, ensuring high-quality education and training.
